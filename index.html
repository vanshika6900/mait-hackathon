Frequently asked questions

Feel free to ask

What is token? How will it benefit you?

A token is an asset for a user which is provided to them as a gift when they provide assistance or volunteer for the organizations.

It would benefit you because as our company grows, the value of token increases so selling it at a later stage would provide greater profits.

What all can I volunteer for?

You can volunteer for various activities to support the organizations.

You can visit the organization and teach the children, organize workshops to spread self-awareness and provide children with the basic necessaties such as clothes and food. For further details you can visit our features's section.

How can you connect to the organization?

You can connect to the organization you want to volunteer for by going under the organization section and choosing the preferred organization.

There you will find all the necessary details helpful to contact the respective organization.
